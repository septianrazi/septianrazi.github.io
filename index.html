Hi, I'm Razi. I'm a software engineer and researcher with a keen eye in making computing feel intriguing and immersive, whether that's a 3D data visualisation floating in mixed reality or uncovering the visual beauty in a dataset.

I've worked across AR/VR/XR research, immersive analytics, Human-Computer Interaction and web development. I'm particularly drawn to problems that sit at the intersection of technology and human perception.

Graduated with three masters degrees from universities in Finland, France and Japan focusing on Extended Reality. Currently building things that push the boundaries of spatial computing and data visualisation.
