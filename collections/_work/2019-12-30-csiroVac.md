Developed immersive data visualisation systems for large-scale agricultural datasets, exploring how complex relational data can be represented and interacted with in 3D environments.

- Built an **interactive 3D graph visualisation** for **complex agricultural pedigree data (45,000+ entries)**, enabling exploration of complex relationships in spatial contexts
- Designed and implemented **novel interaction techniques**, including a bespoke hand gesture detection module for intuitive navigation and manipulation
- Developed systems compatible across **multiple XR platforms** (HoloLens 2, Vive Pro, Oculus Quest), requiring modular and adaptable architecture
- Integrated live and static data sources, supporting more efficient and scalable data exploration workflows

_Focused on making large, complex datasets more explorable through immersive and interactive visualisation techniques._
