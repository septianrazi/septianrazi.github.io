Awarded the Erasmus Mundus scholarship to study Imaging and Lighting for Extended Reality across the University of Eastern Finland, Université Jean Monnet, and Toyohashi University of Technology.

- Studied the intersection of **computer vision, optical science, and XR**, focusing on how light and materials are captured, modelled, and rendered in immersive systems
- Applied **spectral imaging and deep learning techniques** to explore more accurate visual representation in XR environments
- Gained experience across **physically-based rendering and real-time systems**, bridging offline fidelity with interactive performance
- Collaborated in **international research environments**, working across institutions with different technical and research approaches
