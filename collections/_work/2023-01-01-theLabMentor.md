Taught and mentored a weekly class of students with diverse learning needs, including those on the autism spectrum, introducing programming through game development.

- Designed and delivered **structured coding lessons** using game development (Unity, p5.js) to build engagement and progressively develop technical skills
- Adapted teaching approaches to **individual learning styles and needs**, ensuring concepts remained accessible while still challenging
- Created **custom learning plans and activities**, balancing creativity and structure to support long-term skill development
- Guided students through building their own interactive projects, fostering confidence in problem-solving and programming fundamentals

_Focused on making technical concepts accessible through interactive, personalised learning approaches._
