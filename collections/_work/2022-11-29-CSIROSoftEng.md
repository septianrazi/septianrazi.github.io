Developed and evaluated Extended Reality (XR) systems across safety-critical training, immersive analytics, and applied research projects, working at the intersection of software engineering and human-computer interaction.

- Built features for a large-scale VR safety training system, designing interaction flows and UI for simulating hazardous industrial scenarios used by hundreds of trainees
- Designed novel interaction techniques for XR agricultural data visualisation, and co-ran user studies to evaluate how users explore data in 3D environments
- Led development of a cloud-integrated XR laboratory assistant, enabling multi-tasking workflows across desktop and spatial interfaces
- Contributed to applied research projects for industry (e.g. dentistry SMEs), prototyping and evaluating emerging technologies in real-world contexts
- Collaborated closely with researchers, 3D artists, and clients to translate ambiguous requirements into functional, testable systems
