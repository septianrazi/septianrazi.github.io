Designed a concept for a smart water monitoring system aimed at reducing household water consumption through behavioural incentives.

- Proposed a system combining **real-time water usage tracking** with user-facing feedback and analytics
- Designed **gamification features** (goals, rewards, social comparison) to encourage more sustainable usage habits
- Incorporated **social elements**, allowing users to compare and engage with others to drive behavioural change
- Focused on translating environmental challenges into **actionable, user-driven interactions**

- Explored how **data visibility and social dynamics** can influence everyday decision-making
- Balanced technical feasibility with **user motivation and long-term engagement**

Awarded **Best Solution** at the ANU Innovation Challenge.

_Focused on using design and technology to influence sustainable behaviour at scale._
