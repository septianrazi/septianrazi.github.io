**XR · Robotics · Human-Robot Interaction**

Developed a Mixed Reality interface for controlling robots using natural interaction methods, focusing on custom hand gesture recognition and intuitive spatial control.

- Designed and implemented a **custom hand gesture detection module**, extending beyond default HoloLens interactions to enable more expressive and precise robot control
- Translated **hand gestures and voice input** into ROS2-compatible commands, enabling intuitive manipulation of robotic systems
- Built a **communication pipeline** between the HoloLens 2 application and ROS2 nodes via a Flask-based API
- Enabled **multi-device interoperability**, allowing XR devices and multiple robots to operate within a shared system

- Explored how **bespoke interaction techniques** can improve usability compared to traditional controller- or command-based robotics interfaces
- Leveraged prior experience in XR interaction design to develop **more natural and responsive control paradigms**

_Focused on advancing human-robot interaction through custom, embodied input techniques in XR._
