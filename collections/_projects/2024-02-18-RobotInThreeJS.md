**Computer Graphics · Interactive Simulation · WebXR**

Developed an articulated 3D simulation of a quadruped robot in Three.js, modelling joint constraints and movement behaviours inspired by real-world robotics systems.

- Recreated a **quadruped robot (inspired by Spot)** using Three.js primitives, focusing on structural and kinematic accuracy
- Implemented **joint-based movement systems**, simulating realistic articulation and coordinated motion across limbs
- Designed control logic to reflect **physical constraints and capabilities** of robotic joints
- Extended the simulation with **WebXR support**, enabling interaction in immersive VR and AR environments

- Explored how **robotic motion principles** can be represented in lightweight, browser-based 3D systems
- Bridged **kinematics and interactive visualisation**, creating an accessible platform for experimenting with robot behaviour

_Focused on simulating articulated motion and bringing robotics concepts into interactive web-based environments._
