Developed a Mixed Reality experience that translates custom hand gestures into real-time visual effects, inspired by interaction concepts from the Naruto anime.

- Designed and implemented a **custom hand gesture detection system** to trigger different abilities and effects
- Built responsive **particle-based visual systems** to create dynamic, real-time feedback in XR
- Mapped gesture input to **contextual actions**, enabling users to perform complex interactions through natural movement

- Explored how **gesture-driven interaction** can create engaging and expressive user experiences in XR
- Combined technical implementation with **creative design**, using stylised effects to enhance immersion
