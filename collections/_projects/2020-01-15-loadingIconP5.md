Developed a collection of procedural loading animations using p5.js, driven by mathematical transformations and trigonometric functions.

- Generated dynamic animations by applying **trigonometric functions** to control translation, rotation, and scaling of primitive shapes
- Explored how varying **frequency, phase, and amplitude** impacts motion and visual rhythm
- Combined multiple transformations to produce **complex, cyclical animation patterns** from simple primitives
- Experimented with **background opacity and motion trails** to enhance visual depth and aesthetics

- Investigated how mathematical functions can drive **expressive and reusable animation systems**
- Built an extensible playground for experimenting with **procedural motion and generative visuals**

_Focused on creating visually engaging animations through mathematical and procedural techniques._
