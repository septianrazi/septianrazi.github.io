**Data Collection · Analysis · Social Insights**

Developed a web scraping pipeline to analyse engagement patterns within a residential community’s Facebook group.

- Built a **Selenium-based scraper** to collect post data over time, including text, likes, comments, and metadata
- Aggregated and analysed **posting frequency and engagement trends**, identifying shifts during the COVID-19 period
- Performed **basic linguistic analysis**, extracting common terms, popular posts, and frequently used emojis
- Derived insights into how the community **communicates and engages online**

- Worked with **unstructured, real-world data**, requiring custom parsing and cleaning
- Explored how quantitative signals (likes, frequency) relate to **social behaviour and community dynamics**

_Focused on extracting meaningful insights from informal, real-world data sources._
