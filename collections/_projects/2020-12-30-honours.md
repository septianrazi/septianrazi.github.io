**Mixed Reality · Data Visualisation · HCI Research**

Designed and evaluated a Mixed Reality system for exploring large-scale hierarchical pedigree data, addressing the limitations of traditional 2D graph visualisations.

- Developed **3D visualisation and interaction techniques** for navigating complex pedigree structures in immersive environments
- Conducted a **user study (n=20)** to evaluate usability and effectiveness of different 3D graph layouts and interaction methods
- Published findings as a **70-page Honours thesis** and co-authored a paper at _IEEE VR 2021_

- Addressed challenges such as **occlusion, navigation, and visual clutter** in dense 3D graph structures
- Explored how spatial interaction can improve **understanding and exploration of complex relational data**
