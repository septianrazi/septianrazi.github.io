Redesigned and developed the front-end of the CRISPER website, transforming a minimal, unstyled interface into a structured and user-friendly platform.

- Designed a **visual identity and UI system**, defining colour schemes, layout, and overall look and feel
- Created initial **mockups in Figma**, translating design concepts into a consistent front-end implementation
- Built the website using **Bootstrap**, developing responsive layouts and reusable components
- Integrated the front-end with **existing dashboards and project systems**, ensuring a cohesive user experience

- Transformed a **barebones legacy interface** into a functional and accessible web platform
- Balanced rapid development with **design consistency and usability**

_Focused on turning minimal starting points into structured and usable web interfaces._
