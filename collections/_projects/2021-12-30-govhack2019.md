Developed a data-driven platform to explore future job prospects and career growth within geographic constraints during a 48-hour hackathon.

- Aggregated and processed **public datasets** to model regional job trends and opportunities
- Built a **machine learning model** to predict future job availability based on historical and contextual data
- Developed a **full-stack web application** using Flask to present insights through an accessible interface
- Enabled users to explore **career pathways constrained by location**, supporting more informed decision-making

- Designed and delivered a complete product under **tight time constraints (48 hours)**
- Balanced **data modelling and usability**, translating complex predictions into intuitive outputs

Awarded **Runner-Up (International)** and shortlisted for **Western Australia Awards** at GovHack.

_Focused on turning open data into actionable insights through rapid prototyping and full-stack development._
