Conducted an in-depth analysis of multi-year survey data to uncover engagement trends within a residential community.

- Cleaned and integrated **10+ datasets** spanning multiple years, each containing 200+ entries
- Applied **data mining techniques** (including frequent pattern mining) to identify behavioural trends and correlations
- Analysed demographic patterns to detect **disparities in engagement across different groups**

- Identified a **previously overlooked demographic with lower engagement levels**, supported by statistical evidence
- Provided actionable insights that led to **concrete changes in community initiatives and policies**

_Focused on transforming raw survey data into actionable insights that directly influenced real-world decisions._
