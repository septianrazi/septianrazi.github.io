**XR · UI/UX · Safety-Critical Systems**

Contributed to the development of a large-scale VR safety training system, focusing on user interface design and onboarding experiences for high-risk scenarios.

- Designed and implemented **UI/UX elements and interaction flows** to guide users through safety-critical training environments
- Developed the **tutorial and onboarding experience**, ensuring users could quickly understand controls and objectives in immersive contexts
- Built and integrated **core application features and scenes** within a Unity-based XR system
- Collaborated closely with **domain experts and end users**, iterating on designs based on feedback and usability considerations

- Designed for **clarity under cognitive load**, where confusion or misinterpretation could impact training effectiveness
- Balanced **realism and usability**, ensuring immersive scenarios remained accessible to new users

_Focused on designing intuitive onboarding and interaction systems for safety-critical immersive applications._
